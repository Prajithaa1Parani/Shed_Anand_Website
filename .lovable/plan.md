
# Kerala Shed Works — Professional Portfolio Website

## Overview
A stunning, dark-themed portfolio website for your father's Kerala shed construction business. Dark background with gold/amber accents, rich animations throughout, and AI-generated construction images.

## Pages & Features

### 1. **Home (Landing Page)**
- Cinematic hero section with a large shed construction image, animated text reveal (letter-by-letter), and a glowing CTA button
- Animated stats counter (projects completed, years of experience, happy clients)
- Featured works carousel with smooth slide animations
- Testimonial preview with fade-in cards

### 2. **Services**
- Grid of services (Residential Sheds, Commercial Sheds, Custom Designs, Renovation, etc.)
- Each card animates in on scroll with staggered delays
- Hover effects with image zoom and overlay text

### 3. **Works / Gallery**
- Masonry-style project gallery with smooth scale-in animations
- Filter tabs (by project type) with animated transitions
- Click to expand with a lightbox-style modal

### 4. **About**
- Your father's story with parallax-style scroll effects
- Timeline of milestones with animated progress line
- Team/founder section with image and bio

### 5. **Reviews / Testimonials**
- Customer review cards with star ratings
- Animated entrance on scroll
- Quote styling with decorative elements

### 6. **FAQ**
- Accordion-style questions with smooth expand/collapse animations
- Common questions about shed construction, pricing, timelines

### 7. **Contact**
- Contact form (Name, Phone, Email, Message, Project Type dropdown)
- Form sends message via a server function (stored or emailed)
- Animated map placeholder showing Kerala location
- WhatsApp & phone quick-action buttons alongside the form

## Design & Animations
- **Theme**: Dark charcoal/black background, gold (#D4A853) and amber accents, white text
- **Animations**: Framer Motion for scroll-triggered reveals, hover effects, page transitions, staggered card entries, floating elements, and parallax
- **Typography**: Bold display headings, clean body text
- **Images**: AI-generated Kerala shed/construction images using Lovable AI Gateway
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-optimized with hamburger menu

## Navigation
- Sticky header with glassmorphism effect, logo on left, nav links with hover underline animation
- Mobile: animated slide-in drawer menu
- Footer with contact info, quick links, and social media icons
